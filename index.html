Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LAMP membership

LAMP stands for Loyalty Advantage Membership Program. It's a healthcare subscription plan designed to provide affordable, accessible healthcare for individuals, families, and businesses through Doctors Hospital.

Anyone can sign up for LAMP. We offer plans for individuals, families, and businesses of all sizes.

No, LAMP is not insurance. It's a direct primary care membership that can complement your existing insurance or serve as standalone coverage for primary care needs.

Your LAMP benefits can be used at Doctors Hospital and all affiliated clinics in our network.

Membership fees are paid monthly via credit card or bank draft for your convenience.

You can contact our support team at any time to make changes to your plan or cancel your membership.
